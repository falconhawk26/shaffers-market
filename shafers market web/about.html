About Shaffer's Market

Shaffer’s Market is a family‑owned neighborhood grocery store rooted in Akron, Ohio. For decades, we’ve focused on fresh meats, quality produce, and friendly service.

Our butcher counter is the heart of the store—custom cuts, special orders, and advice on what to cook tonight. We believe in knowing our customers by name and helping them feed their families well.

Our story

From the early days as a small corner market to today’s full‑service grocery, Shaffer’s has grown with the community. Many of our customers have been shopping here for generations.

What makes us different

  • Real butcher‑cut meats, not pre‑packaged.
  • Local and seasonal produce when available.
  • Friendly staff who actually help you find what you need.
  • Weekly specials that make quality affordable.
